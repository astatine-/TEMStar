IEEE TEMS TEMSTar Award Nominations

The IEEE Technology and Engineering Management Society (TEMS) is proud to announce the TEMSTar Awards, recognizing outstanding professionals in the fields of technology and engineering management. We invite you to nominate exemplary leaders and managers who have made significant contributions to their fields.

Award Categories

Technology Leader of the Year

This award celebrates a visionary leader who has made notable technological advancements, driven innovation, and inspired teams to achieve remarkable outcomes.

Nominate for Technology Leader of the Year

Engineering Manager of the Year

Recognizing an individual who has demonstrated exceptional skills in managing engineering projects, leading teams, and delivering high-impact results while promoting ethical engineering practices.

Who Can Nominate?

We welcome nominations from all professionals within the technology and engineering fields. Whether you are a colleague, supervisor, or team member, if you know someone who embodies excellence in leadership or management, we encourage you to submit a nomination.

Selection Process

All nominations will be reviewed by a distinguished panel of IEEE TEMS experts. Winners will be announced in December 2024, and the awards will be presented at a special IEEE TEMS event.
