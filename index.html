Announcing the Launch of the TEMStar Award

Presented by IEEE

IEEE is proud to introduce the TEMStar Award, a prestigious recognition for outstanding contributions in the field of Technology and Engineering Management.

About the TEMStar Award

The TEMStar Award is designed to acknowledge individuals and organizations that have significantly advanced the practice, education, and development of engineering management principles and leadership. It highlights innovation, commitment, and impact in this evolving field.

Eligibility Criteria

  • IEEE membership or affiliation.
  • Significant contributions in the areas of technology and engineering management.
  • Innovative leadership and practical application of management principles.

Nomination Process

Nominees can be submitted by peers, colleagues, or IEEE members. The nomination process will begin on October 1, 2024 and end on December 1, 2024. Details about the submission process will be available soon on the official IEEE website.
